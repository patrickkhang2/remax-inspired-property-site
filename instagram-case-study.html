The Challenge

Growing an organic following from scratch on a platform dominated by paid promotion and established creators. The goal: prove that strategic, authentic content could break through the noise without a single dollar spent on ads.

The Strategy

Every piece of content was built around a clear framework: hook within the first second, deliver value in the middle, and close with a reason to follow. I studied trending audio, optimal posting times, and audience behavior to maximize algorithmic distribution.
