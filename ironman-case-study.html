Case Study

RE/MAX NZ Blog

A UX and SEO-focused blog concept designed to drive organic traffic and establish thought leadership in New Zealand real estate.

Type Blog Concept
Focus UX + SEO
Industry Real Estate
Role Strategist & Designer

The Brief

RE/MAX NZ needed a content hub that could rank organically, educate home buyers and sellers, and position the brand as a trusted authority in the NZ property market.

The Approach

I designed a blog architecture built around SEO pillars — targeting high-intent search queries that NZ property seekers actually use. Every design decision was rooted in UX research: clear hierarchy, scannable layouts, and conversion-optimized CTAs.

Key Design Principles

Clean typography. Generous whitespace. Trust signals throughout. Mobile-first design ensuring the reading experience works beautifully across all devices. Content structured for both human readers and search engine crawlers.
