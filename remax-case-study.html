RE/MAX NZ Case Study

Home Projects

Real Estate Blog Designed for Engagement & Lead Generation

UX + Marketing + SEO focused web project inspired by RE/MAX New Zealand.

🎯 The Problem

Real estate content is often fragmented, difficult to navigate, and lacks engagement. Users need clearer insights into the NZ property market with better structure and readability.

💡 The Solution

I designed a clean, responsive blog layout focused on: - User experience (easy navigation) - Content clarity (structured blog cards) - Marketing conversion (strong CTAs like “Speak to an Agent”)

🧠 My Approach

I combined marketing thinking with front-end development by: - Using grid-based layouts for readability - Designing CTA-driven sections - Structuring content around SEO-friendly blog topics

📈 Outcome

The final website simulates a real estate content hub that improves engagement, encourages user interaction, and demonstrates how digital design supports marketing goals.
